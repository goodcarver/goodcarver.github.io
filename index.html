the steps we have to take

Spotify • Apple Music • Amazon Music • YouTube Music • Tidal • Deezer • Pandora • iHeartRadio • SoundCloud • Bandcamp

Shaped over four years, this collection mirrors the shift many lived between 2021 and 2025: from isolation to a renewed appreciation for connection. These songs distill observations of the natural world, perplexing human behavior, vulnerable reflection, and glimmers of hope and gratitude into heartfelt vocals and rich instrumentation, the product of organic collaboration among kindred spirits.
